Sheet Metal Camera Bracket

My Design For Manufacturing class covered various methods of manufacturing. This included the manufacture of sheet metal products. Our assigned project was to design a sheet metal bracket to hold a camera at a specific angle, centered on a specific area of interest. This was based on a possible assembly that could be found in the manufacture of diabetic glucose monitoring strips. The bracket needed to be designed with consideration to both tolerancing and sheet metal design specifications.
