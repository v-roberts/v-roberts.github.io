Aquapus

My multidisciplinary senior design class started with a two week long kickoff project to design a 3.5mm switch operated toy for Reach Services in Terre Haute, IN. My team chose to design a toy that would appeal to children ages 3-7 years old. We made sure to include a lot of visual stimulus with the shiny beaded legs, motion, and lights. We anticipate that this toy would teach cause and effect as well as help increase the user's visual attention span.

We have established a good starting point for actually making a finalized iteration of this product. (Fall 2019)
