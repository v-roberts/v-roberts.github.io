Eighth Iteration Dragon Curve



I designed this for my friend who is a mathematician. I know she loves the intersection of math and art. So, I made a 3D version of her favorite fractal: a dragon curve. I designed the dragon curve to fill the 3D printer's build-plate, creating a very eye-catching conversation piece / pencil holder. I made sure to extrude the different iterations to different heights so that they are visible. Its dimensions are 147 X 183 X 72 mm. (Spring 2018)
