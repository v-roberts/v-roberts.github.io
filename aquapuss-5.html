Feature Spotlight:
Aquapuss Base

Early in the process, we all came up with different concepts for what the base of the toy should look like. I favored bases that polygonal cross-sections so that the toy would not roll when knocked over.

The first concept has such an extreme angle to the sides that it would hopefully roll in small circles if knocked over. The other two concepts have polygonal cross sections (square and a triangle lofted up to a circle) that would not roll if knocked over.
