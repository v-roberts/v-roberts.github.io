FEATURE SPOTLIGHT: Snap Tab

The snap tabs protrude from the main body. When the halves are folded together, the snap slots can be folded over the tabs and the small raised portion keeps the slot stationary. This method is used to secure the halves of the container together and the flaps to the sides of the container.
