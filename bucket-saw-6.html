Feature Spotlight: Bucket Saw Cutting Instructions

In production, this image would be printed out to a 1:1 scale and could be taped to a bucket lid to indicate where to cut. Cutting on the dotted lines with the jig saw that the user already possesses will put a perfectly shaped hole in the lid.
