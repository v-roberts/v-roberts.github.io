Connector Module

The additional module we created has some common connectors that weren't required on the power module: SD card, HDMI, and audio jacks.

We envisioned this product superseding computer towers due to its customizability and this would be one of the most commonly asked for units as these are things commonly used on computer towers.

The connectors that interface with the lower modules when they are pushed together are visible here.
