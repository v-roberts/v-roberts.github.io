Electronics Enclosure

Module Bottom

The module bottom is designed to be injection molded with a core pin to create the spaces for the connectors.

We designed it to be thin to cut down on the cost of material and to keep the height of the tower of modules as low as possible. There are raised lips on all four edges to help position the bottom during assembly.

There are extra screw bosses to connect a PCB to so the connectors can be wired up.
