Stamped Dragon Size

The stamped dragon is ~2.25 inches in length; this is due to the largest dimension on the egg half it would be stored in being ~2.5 inches.

The small size of the stamped dragon is a choking hazard concern for smaller children. However, due to the reason stated above, no part can exceed ~2.5 inches in the largest dimension and some parts must necessarily be larger than others in order to create a correctly proportioned dragon.
