Modified Remote Control Car

For this project, I designed interchangeable joysticks for an RC car and controller modified to be used by a person with motor skill disabilities. This RC car, controller, joysticks, and track were designed for Reach Services in Terre Haute, IN. They are still in use today, continuing to be checked out by various children in the Terre Haute community.
