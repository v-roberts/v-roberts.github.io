Feature Spotlight: Power Module Feet

Though having a flat bottom on the power module may keep the stack from sliding, we chose to have four small feet to keep the base from dragging on whatever surface it is set on. This keeps the power module up and away from any liquid that may be spilled by it. If there does turn out to be concerns around slipping, rubber grippers could easily be added to the feet.
