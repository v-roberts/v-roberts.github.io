Electronics Enclosure

Full Assembly

This is the vision for what the product would look like if it were mass produced.

The aluminum is brushed which will diffuse the light from the indicator LEDs and disguise any imperfections. The plastic is textured which will give more feedback when sliding the module together and will help disguise imperfections. The edges are rounded to keep the user safe and make the injection molding and extrusion dies last longer.
