Final Product: Dragon / Sea Monster

This toy is one of the two we manufactured for Reach. It has smaller fasteners that require more dexterity and force to use. This is intentional as it is meant to cater to users who may have skills beyond the other toy we made.

It has parts to become a dragon (wings zipped on, legs buttoned on, and a tail attached with loops) and a sea monster (fins buttoned on and finned tail attached with loops), with some shared parts (horns snapped on and nose attached with velcro).
