Bucket Saw Attachment

To make the most universally adaptable bucket saw, we designed the top plate to connect to the lid of a five gallon bucket. This allowed us to forgo having to reinvent a way to attach what is essentially a lid to the bucket and gives the user access to an easily operated lid system they are familiar with.

The saw is connected to the plate by placing the saw with its shoe in the footprint on the plate and screwing the bracket around it.
