Case Study: Multidisciplinary Senior Design

My multidisciplinary senior design project was to make an adapted toy for the Reach Services Clinic in Terre Haute, IN. My team started with 18 toy concepts gleaned from research into the available adapted toys and current popular toys. We then gathered feedback from industry resources (Adaptabilities, Adaptive Tech Solutions, and Enabling Devices), personal contacts, and the therapists at Reach Services.

We are now in the process of making the second prototype and testing it. (Fall 2019)
