Scent Dispenser

FEATURE SPOTLIGHT: Snap Slot

The clasp feature on the scent dispenser consists of a slot and a tab. The slot extends from a living hinge and has a filleted bottom edge to allow fingertip purchase when opening the container. This method is used to secure the halves of the container together and the flaps to the sides of the container.
